Linkedin Recruiment Process



Recruitment Process:-


☛ LinkedIn conducts 5-6 rounds to select freshers as SDE in their organisation.
☛ Online Round
☛ Technical Round 1
☛ Technical Round 2
☛ Technical Round 3
☛ Technical Round 4
☛ System Design Round
☛ HR Round


Academic Criteria :-
☛ 60 percent or above in B.Tech, Class X and XII.
☛ No backlogs at the time of interview


Written Round:-
☛ Online round contains 2-3 coding problems for which time duration is 1 hr.
☛ Sometimes this round also include MCQs which are mainly from data structures, algorithms, oops, dbms, Networking and Linux.


Technical Rounds :-
☛ The students who clear the written round are called for Technical Interview.
☛ To clear this round you should be clear with your basics.
☛ You should be prepared with Data structures and Algorithms, Operating System, networking and dbms.
☛ Students from branches other than CS should prepare for other two subjects related to their branch.
☛ Sometimes these rounds may also be online coding rounds.
☛ They also ask questions from resume.
☛ They frequently ask questions about your previous projects.
☛ You may be asked puzzles in this round.
☛ To be prepared for puzzles you can practice from our Puzzles section.


System Design Rounds:-
☛ In this round you can expect designing based problems such as to design a file server system.


HR Round:-
Sometimes they only ask HR questions like
1. Tell me about Yourself
2. Your family background?
3. Projects mentioned in Resume
4. Problems faced during projects
5. Some real life based problems


Questions Asked in LinkedIn:-
☛ Closest Strings
☛ Longest Palindromic Subsequence
☛ Find first and last occurrence of x
☛ Pair with greatest product in array
☛ Merge Two Sorted Arrays
☛ Search a node in BST (Function Problem)
☛ How is the data structure of System R different from the relational structure?
☛ What are the differences between paging and segmentation?
☛ What are some pros and cons of a command line interface?
☛ What is time-stamping?
☛ Describe the Buddy system of memory allocation
☛ What is the Translation Lookaside Buffer (TLB)?
